Words with f, ff, ph


Convention/Grammar

The letter "f":

  • Makes a sound like blowing out a candle.
  • Examples:fish,fun,frog

The double "ff":

  • Makes the same sound as "f", but it's double the fun!
  • Often comes at the end of words after a short vowel sound.
  • Examples:puff,stuff,fluff

The tricky "ph":

  • Looks different but sounds just like "f".
  • It's like "p" and "h" are playing dress-up as "f".
  • Examples:phone,elephant,photo

Remember our special rules:

  • Words often end with "ff" after a short vowel sound.
  • "Ph" always makes the "f" sound, no matter where it is in a word.

More examples:

  • Frog (f)
  • Stuff (ff)
  • Dolphin (ph)
  • Fluffy (f and ff)
  • Elephant (ph)
